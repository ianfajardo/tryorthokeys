## What Is the MT3 Profile?

MT3 is a sculpted, high-profile keycap shape co-designed by Drop and Matt3o, modeled after the IBM beamspring terminal keyboards of the 1970s. Each row has a different angle, the tops are deeply scooped, and the overall feel is taller and chunkier than the OEM or Cherry profile sets that ship on most stock keyboards.

Three things made it iconic:

1. **Comfortable to type on** — the deep scoops cradle your fingertips, so you naturally hit the center of each cap.
2. **Durable** — most MT3 sets were either doubleshot ABS or dye-sub PBT, both of which wear well over years of use.
3. **Distinctive** — once you've seen MT3, you can spot it across a room. The tall retro silhouette is unmistakable.

## What Happened to MT3 in 2026

MT3 was effectively a Drop-exclusive profile. Drop owned the tooling, ran the group buys, and handled distribution. When [Drop's ecommerce store closed March 31, 2026](/posts/drop-ecommerce-store-closing-march-31-keyboard-club), the production pipeline for new MT3 sets effectively closed with it. The few remaining listings online — Amazon, manufacturer direct pages, third-party storefronts — are mostly running on leftover stock. New colorways aren't being produced. **If you want MT3 in 2026, you're hunting for what's left, not shopping a live category.**

## What to Buy Instead: Sculpted Profiles That Are Actively Sold

This is where most readers should focus. Several other sculpted, high-profile keycap profiles cover the same "tall, retro, comfortable to type on" niche that MT3 occupied, and they're being actively manufactured in 2026 with multiple retailers and active stock. Here are the four worth knowing.

### SA Profile — The Closest Analog (Most Available)

**SA** is Signature Plastics' classic sculpted, tall profile. It predates MT3, and many people argue it has a better-defined typing sound and feel. The good news for you: SA is *everywhere*. We carry affiliate links across multiple retailers for it.

- **[Pimp My Keyboard](http://pimpmykeyboard.com?afmc=5w&utm_campaign=5w&utm_source=leaddyno&utm_medium=affiliate)** — direct from Signature Plastics, the manufacturer. The deepest catalog. Sets like SA-P Snow Cap, Ice Cap, Beyond, and Granite all have ortho add-on kits available.
- **[KBDfans](https://kbdfans.com/r?id=ifs0u0)** — wide SA selection at fair prices.
- **[Kono](https://sovrn.co/rgjoybp)** — SA Granite, SA Magica, SA Espresso, all with ortho kits.
- **[Epomaker](https://epomaker.com?sca_ref=10950542.Ki2iuo7P0N)** — newer SA group buy reproductions.
- **[KPrepublic](https://kprepublic.com)** — budget SA dye-sub options.

For an ortholinear board specifically, look at the SA section on our [keycaps page](/keycaps#kono) — the Kono SA Granite and SA Magica sets in particular are popular Planck/Preonic pairings.

### MTNU Profile — Matt3o's Successor to MT3

If you specifically loved MT3 because of Matt3o's design hand, **MTNU is his newer profile and the spiritual replacement**. It's still sculpted and PBT, but medium-height instead of MT3's tall stack — easier to type on for hands used to standard profiles, and arguably better-engineered.

MTNU is sold via GMK group buys through several enthusiast vendors. We don't currently have affiliate relationships with these, but for completeness:

- [NovelKeys](https://novelkeys.com/products/gmk-mtnu-granite-keycaps) — main US vendor for MTNU group buys
- [Oblotzky Industries](https://oblotzky.industries/collections/gmk-mtnu-keycap-sets) — European vendor, ships globally
- [prototypist](https://prototypist.net/) — UK vendor

MTNU is the right pick if you want the closest-to-MT3 design philosophy but on a profile that's actually being made today.

### KAT Profile — Uniform-ish Tall Profile

**KAT** is Keyreative's profile — sculpted but with less row-to-row variance than SA or MT3, and a smoother sphere on top. It's a strong middle-ground if MT3 felt too aggressive but Cherry felt too flat.

- [Keyreative](https://keyreative.store/collections/kat) — direct from the manufacturer

### MOA, DSA, and Other Profiles Worth Knowing

If you're open to non-sculpted options:

- **DSA** — uniform-height (not sculpted), every key the same shape. The most ortho-friendly profile in existence. - **DCS** — sculpted but shorter than SA/MT3. Pimp My Keyboard offers blank DCS sets in PBT.
- **MOA** — newer profile from Epomaker, sculpted high. Some sets ship with their own keyboards.
